Rotisserie Chicken

Description

Create nutritious and delicious Rotisserie Chicken meal whith the below ingredients and instructions. This meal is low in fat and high in protein!

Ingredients

  • store-bought rotisserie chicken
  • sugar-free sweet and spicy sauce
  • seasoning salt
  • olive oil
  • zucchini

Steps

  1. set a large non-stick pan to medium heat on a stovetop
  2. while the pan is heating, slice the zucchini into 1/4" slices
  3. apply a light layer of olive oil to the base of the pan
  4. add the sliced zucchini to the pan
  5. add seasoning salt to the zucchini, ensuring good coverage
  6. pick rotisserie chicken to separate the cuts of meat
  7. let zucchini brown before flipping
  8. pour zucchini onto plate with chicken and serve with sweet and spicy sauce
  9. enjoy!
