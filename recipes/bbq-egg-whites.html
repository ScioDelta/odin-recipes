BBQ Egg Whites

Description

Create nutritious and delicious BBQ Egg whites whith the below ingredients and instructions. This meal is low in fat and high in protein!

Ingredients

  • liguid egg whites
  • sugar-free bbq sauce
  • seasoning salt
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. set a large non-stick pan to medium heat on a stovetop
  2. apply a light layer of olive oil to the base of the pan
  3. add 100g of liquid egg whites to the pan
  4. wait for the egg whites to harden and start to slowly bubble
  5. add seasoning salt as the egg heats
  6. flip the egg whites with a spatula and let the egg cook through
  7. use the spatula to slide the egg onto a plate and drizzle with bbq sauce
  8. enjoy!
